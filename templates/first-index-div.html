Bigfoot the legendary creature that is said to inhabit remote forests, mainly in the Pacific Northwest region of North America. Also known as Sasquatch, Bigfoot is typically described as a large, hairy, bipedal creature, standing between 6 to 10 feet tall and weighing around 500 pounds. It is said to be extremely elusive and has never been captured or scientifically proven to exist, leading many to dismiss it as a myth or legend.
